How can you use the staging area to make sure you have one commit per logical change?
- you can put in the staging area only one logical change at the time commit then move to the next one.
- keep other changes in the working directory. 



What are some situations when branches would be helpful in keeping your history organized? How would branches help?
- branches are helpful when you want to do something new, but keep the original work untacked. 
- this way you can switch between branches easily and without confusing versions together.
